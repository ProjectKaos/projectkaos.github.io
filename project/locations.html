Locations

Learn about the islands and cities we visit. Click “View Details” to see more notes and observations.

Foosha Village

Luffy's hometown, peaceful and lively.

Foosha Village resembles a typical rural community, with rustic wooden houses, docks and a few windmills. It is located to the east of the Goa Kingdom capital.

Shells Town

Town under Marine control, introduces key conflicts.

Shells Town is located in the Yotsuba Island Region of East Blue. The town is distinguished by its skyline of power plant-shaped towers, each bristling with cannons. It serves as the third setting in the Romance Dawn Arc.

Baratie

Floating restaurant, home of Sanji.

The Baratie is an ocean-going restaurant run by Zeff. It is located in the Sambas Region (サンバス海域, Sanbasu Kaiiki?) of the East Blue, close to the Paradise half of the Grand Line.
