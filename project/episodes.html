One Piece Episodes

Here’s my journey through One Piece, episode by episode. Click “Read More” to see my full reactions and notes!

Episode 1: I’m Luffy!

Luffy starts his journey and meets new friends.

I love how nonchalant non caring Luffy is. Bro does not care he’s gonna do and say whatever he wants, does not care how anyone else cares or reacts to his behavior. What’s the deal with the whole rubber devil fruit thing?

Episode 2: Enter Zoro

Luffy meets Zoro, the swordsman.

Roronoa Zoro has serious aura, probably my favorite character so far. Marine lord is cool (shoutout the hand axe), his son is annoying af. Luffy’s matrix rubber bullet deflection move was crazy.

Episode 3: Buggy’s Crew

First antagonist appears.

Three sword style is crazy work. Ah so devil fruit gives special powers, how many are there? People keep talking about “The grand line”. Zoro wants to be the best swordsman of all time, he says “if he has to give it up”, foreshadowing? Roro’Aura’ Zoro. Who’s the orange haired chick?

Episode 4: Adventure Continues

Exploring more of the world and crew goals.

I still don’t know who this orange haired chick is. Like a side story shown at the same time? Take away your ability to swim? What a dumb consequence of eating a power giving fruit in a highly naval world lol. Shanks and his crew (especially the green fat guy) has serious aura. Wait, so a smokescreen just TP’s you miles away?? Did- did shanks just solo a sea serpent?? I still don’t know who the orange haired chick is.
