Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some questions and answers about my experience watching One Piece and navigating this site.

Why am I watching One Piece?

I’m watching One Piece because a friend convinced me to start it, and I wanted to document my first-time reactions and thoughts for fun.

Will you spoil the story?

No, I try to avoid major spoilers and focus on my personal reactions as I watch each episode.

Can I contribute my own thoughts?

Eventually, I might allow guest comments or submissions, but for now it’s just my journey.

How often is the site updated?

I update the site as I watch new episodes, so the frequency depends on my viewing pace.

Is this your first time watching One Piece?

Yes, this is my very first time watching One Piece all the way through. I made this site to track my reactions, favorite moments, and how my opinions change as the story grows.

Are you watching sub or dub?

I am watching the English voice acted version right now, but I might switch to sub at some point depending on how things go. I am mostly focused on enjoying the story and characters.

How often do you update the site?

I try to update whenever I finish a new batch of episodes. Sometimes that is a few times a week, sometimes a little slower when school gets busy.

Will this site contain spoilers?

Yes, there will be spoilers for the episodes that I have already watched. I try not to spoil future arcs on purpose, but if you are behind what I have seen, just be careful.

Who is your favorite character so far?

This changes a lot as I keep watching. Right now I really love Zoro.
