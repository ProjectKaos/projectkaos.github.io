Important Locations

Explore the islands, towns, and seas I encounter along the journey.

The peaceful home village of our hero, Luffy.

Dawn Island is an island located in the East Blue and where the Goa Kingdom is situated. It is the home island of Monkey D. Luffy and the starting point of his journey into piracy, as well as the home island of his father, Monkey D. Dragon and grandfather, Monkey D. Garp. This is also the adopted home of Portgas D. Ace after the death of his mother and the home of Sabo and his family.

View Locations

Where the adventure really begins for the Straw Hats.

Foosha Village (literally meaning "Windmill Village") is a little port village that is located on Dawn Island and is part of Goa Kingdom. It is the first setting of the Romance Dawn Arc, and is where the story of One Piece starts from.
